# 🎨 Story Production Workflow

Follow these steps to ensure every story reaches your audience with the highest quality.

## Step 1: Ideation (Draft)
*   **Action**: Click "NEW STORY" in the Dashboard.
*   **Goal**: Create a title and choose a category.
*   **Result**: Story appears in the "Stories" table with a **DRAFT** badge.

## Step 2: Scripting & Assets
*   **Action**: Move story to **SCRIPTED** when the text is ready.
*   **Audio/Video**: Use the "Upload" tab to upload your final voiceover/video file.
*   **Thumbnail**: Upload a high-res (1280x720) image.

## Step 3: Production (Recording & Editing)
*   **Action**: Update status to **RECORDING** then **EDITING** as you work through the media.
*   **Verification**: Ensure the "Duration" is updated in the story details.

## Step 4: Quality Check (Review)
*   **Action**: Move story to **REVIEW**.
*   **Review Tab**: Open the "Review" tab in the dashboard.
*   **Approval**: Click the green **Approve** button. This moves the story to **APPROVED**.

## Step 5: Publishing
*   **Action**: Go to the **Publish** tab.
*   **YouTube**: Select your approved story.
*   **Metadata**: The app will auto-fill the title, description, and tags for you.
*   **Go Live**: Click **Push to YouTube**.

## Step 6: Post-Release (Published)
*   **Monitoring**: Check the "Dashboard" tab after 24 hours to see real-time views and likes fetched from YouTube.
